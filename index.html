Our Story...

Always a student, Janet has immersed herself in the ancient practices of yoga for over thirty years. As a global yoga teacher, she shares the teachings from the heart. Through curiosity, devotion, and dedication, she creates a unique appoach to living yoga.
Janet's commitment to her practice remains unswerving, and she continues to evolve, enriching her understanding of yoga's profound philosophy and its transformative impact on both body and soul. Together, we embark on this enduring voyage, guided by the wisdom and grace that Janet imparts, weaving the timeless threads of yoga into the fabric of our lives.
